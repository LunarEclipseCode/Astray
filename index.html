How to play Astray

Navigate the ball through the maze to reach the exit. Each level's exit is randomly positioned at one of the four corners.

Avoid the red balls. Colliding with them will restart the level.

When a level restarts, the maze layout will change, but the exit will remain in the same corner.

Controls: Use WASD, arrow keys, HJKL, a controller, or touch and drag on the screen (for mobile devices) to move the ball.
